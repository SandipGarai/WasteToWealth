🎯 AI & Computer Vision
Multi-Modal Imaging: Drone imagery at 50m-200m altitudes, satellite temporal analysis, and ground-level high-resolution photography.

Deep Learning: Transfer learning from YOLO architectures with data augmentation, 80/10/10 train/validation/test splits.

Performance Target: >85% accuracy in BD/NBD classification under real-world conditions.
🧬 Microbial Biotechnology
Isolation Strategy: Multi-layer sampling (0-45cm depth) across seasonal variations from Jharkhand dumpsites.

Characterization: Morphological, biochemical, and molecular identification using 16S rRNA sequencing.

Consortium Development: Synergistic interaction studies for optimized dual organic/plastic degradation.
🌾 Agricultural Validation
Field Trials: Randomized complete block design across multiple soil types and crop varieties.

Safety Assessment: Microplastic detection, heavy metal screening, and pathogen analysis.

Economic Analysis: Comprehensive cost-benefit evaluation with market integration studies.
⛓️ Blockchain Integration
Platform: Hyperledger Fabric with smart contracts for automated validation.

Data Management: Immutable storage of processing data, quality metrics, and traceability records.

Multi-Location: Synchronized operations across distributed processing sites.
